Leading from the front

Mr. Ravi AV has his Masters Degree in Foreign Trade. Having more than 20 years of experience in LED Industry, Mr. Ravi, today interfaces with all media of lighting and energy efficiency in the field of commercial lighting, domestic lighting, automotive lighting, LED Video Walls, Bio-fuels and many more. With deep sense of belonging to this industry,
Mr. Ravi has contributed immensely to the formulation of LED Standards in the country by brining global expertise to various Forums. As an active member of various Govt. Committees in the area of lighting at Research Design & Standards Organisation (RDSO), Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE), Ministry of Power (MOP), Solar Energy Center (SEC), Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE), he has contributed regularly in LED field, besides his active involvement in popularizing the qualitative usage of LEDs in the country. He is one of the key members who contributed his services towards aggregating demand for LED products in India. Has been a regular Speaker at various Universities and Engineering Colleges including Mewar University, Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi College of Engineering, to mention a few. Mr. Ravi has recently been on board of M/s. TQN Industries Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi besides started his own venture M/s. Go-LED Tech Pvt. Ltd. in 2016 at Bangalore. Prior to that, he had served in Government as well as in Japanese MNCs like Sumitomo Corporation and Nichia Corporation.
